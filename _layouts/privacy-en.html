Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21st, 2025

CBA Cloud ("we", "us", or "our") operates the platform available at www.cbacloud.com ("Website"). This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal information from users of our platform.

By accessing or using our Website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue your use of the Website.

Personal Information We Collect

When users create an account on our platform, we automatically collect limited personal information. This includes:

  • IP address
  • Geographic location (derived from IP)

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any contact details directly unless explicitly provided via other contact mechanisms.

How We Use This Information

The information we collect is used solely to ensure secure access to the platform and to monitor suspicious activity for security and compliance purposes. We do not use this data for marketing, analytics, or profiling.

No Cookies or Tracking Tools

CBA Cloud does not use cookies, web beacons, pixels, or any third-party tracking technologies on this website.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal information to any third parties. All data remains within CBA Cloud and is handled internally by our infrastructure.

Data Storage and Retention

We store personal data securely and retain it only as long as necessary. If a user requests deletion of their account or data, we will permanently remove their personal information from our systems.

Data Transfers

Your data is not transferred outside of your jurisdiction. All data is processed and stored in accordance with Portuguese and European data protection laws.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections or deletion of that data, and withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the contact page of our website.

Minors

CBA Cloud is not intended for use by minors. Our platform is designed for adult professionals and business entities. We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. We encourage users to review this page periodically to stay informed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please reach out to us via the contact page of our website.
